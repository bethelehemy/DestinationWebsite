✧ Places to Visit ✧

  • Tokyo Skytree
    • If you want to get a beautiful view of the Shibuya skyline, the Tokyo Skytree is
      definitely a place that you want to visit! At the top of this tower, you will see
      breathtaking views of the city; A view that you will never forget!
  • Akihabara
    • If you are a fan of anime culture, add Akihabara to your list! This city is a popular
      shopping district that holds tons of restaurants, games, merch, and more. All this
      creates an exciting and engaging atmosphere!
  • Ginza District
    • The Ginza District is definitely popular within Japan but is internationally known as
      one of the best shopping districts in the world. With an innumerable amount of stores
      with various brands and cafes, and the cultural vibrance, it is surely worth a visit!
